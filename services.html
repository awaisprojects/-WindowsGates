Window Security Bars

Window security bars stop intruders from breaking into homes and businesses. The bars deter thieves visually. Any dwelling with window bars will be harder to break into, and criminals will look for an unsecured house instead. Window bars are usually made of steel or aluminum, though heavier bars made of wrought iron are available. Some security bars have additional features, like alarm triggers or removable parts.

Removable Security Bars

Quick-release, or expandable security bars, swing outward to allow the homeowner to escape in case of emergency. They remove and reattach quickly as long as the angle bracket remains on the window frame.

window guards

Window guards not only keep burglars out, but also keep children from falling out of or exiting windows in your home. The guards are made out of metal, contain two or more brackets for securing the guard in place and are removable if you decide you no longer want or need them.
