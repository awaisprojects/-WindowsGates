Since expansive views are common where I build decks, I'm frequently asked about railings that won't obstruct those views. Before I discuss the installation of cable railings, I'll offer a couple of words of caution. Check with the local building authority before suggesting them to your clients, as some jurisdictions don't allow horizontal railings. The concern is the potential for people, particularly children, to climb over them.
